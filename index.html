aventure.


The legendary aventure game was born on a calculator in early high school. Badly written and frustrating to play, it was a fantastic learning experience doomed to failure. The great calculator crash calamity of the early 2010's necessitated a rewrite. Then came python. Still badly written, still frustrating to play, aventuregame 2.0 was born. It lasted awhile but as its code base grew it became harder and harder to maintain (not really, I'm just lazy). And the python-based aventure game was stuck in a terminal. Porting it to the web proved to be a challenge. Fast-forward to the present day. Children are smiling in the park. Albatrosses are flying overhead. Dogs are barking merrily. And AVENTUREGAME IS REBORN. Completely redesigned from scratch, and rewritten in javascript. This version will likely also be badly written and frustrating to play, but hey, such is life.
